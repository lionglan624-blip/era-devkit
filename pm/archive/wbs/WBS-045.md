## Character Notes

**Meiling's personality for dialogue:**
- Laid-back, easy-going
- Gate guard responsibility (but a bit careless)
- Mix of polite and casual speech
- Honest, apologizes when wrong

**Sample speech patterns:**
- 「あら、私について来てもこっちには何もなくてよ？」
- 「済みません、お嬢様〜。」
- First person: 私
- Endings: 〜わ、〜よ (mixed)
